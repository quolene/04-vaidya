04-Vaidya

Built this for my mother. She gets medical reports. She cannot understand them. Neither can most people who didn't go to medical school.
Most health apps assume you already know what you're looking at. Vaidya assumes you don't.

Upload a report — USG, blood test, X-Ray — and get an explanation the way a doctor would give one if they had time.
Plain language. What it means, what to eat, what to avoid, how urgent. Switch to Hindi. Switch to an Indian diet plan. It remembers your family, not just you.

Live: https://quolene.github.io/04-vaidya?v=2

Multi-member profiles with PIN login, medicine tracking, appointment calendar, doctor finder by pincode, in-app chat that knows your conditions. 
More features than I planned. Turns out once you solve one problem in a household health context, six more appear immediately.

Learned: scope creep is sometimes just accurate problem mapping.
